   The King
   
   The King is the most important piece in chess. it can move 1 square in all direction. it is 
   also the most vulnerable.When the king is in check the player cannot move unless the king is safe
   or guarded by another piece.
	
   The Queen
   
   The Queen is the most powerful piece in chess because it can move vertically,horizontally or
   diagonally like the rook and bishop in any amount of squares.
