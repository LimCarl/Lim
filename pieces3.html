   The Knight
   
   The knight is a Unique piece that can move in an L shape. It can cross over 
   any piece in the game without capturing it. It resembles a horse figure.
	
   The Pawn
   
   The pawn is considered the weakest piece in the game due to it having limited
   movement and only being able to move forward and and capture the enemy only if 
   they are in another line.
